Metabolic is an action agency for societal transformation.

About Us

Metabolic was born out of a desire to realize epic, groundbreaking projects that actively transition the world to a more equitable, sustainable state. We integrate existing technologies to create maximum systemic benefit.

Metabolic is a tech development company that creates integrated systems for cities and agriculture. We are a consulting company that supports organizations in transitioning to sustainable metabolism.

We develop sensing and automation systems for food production and household resource flows, mobile utilities for off-grid activities, and sustainability transition strategies for companies and governments.

How we work

Metabolic is powered by a cooperative of multi-disciplinary entrepreneurs who analyze, design, experiment, and build disruptive solutions. We aim to regenerate natural capital, strengthen social ties among people, and empower organizations to transition to a new future.

We work through a data-driven process that takes a systems approach to developing solutions. We integrate existing technologies and stakeholders into our design of systems, which we take from ideation through execution.

Philosophy

We believe in democratizing technology and design solutions to be affordable and accessible. Most of our work is published open source to encourage replication and fast adoption.

We need to move beyond incremental change, which requires continuously researching, prototyping, and implementing ideas that result in holistic and replicable systems for a sustainable society.

We take jobs and build products based on impact, not prestige. We measure a strategy by its ability to scale and to sustain itself, not by its profitability. We believe in taking action, getting things done in the real world, and learning rapidly from mistakes.

Services

Analyze

We translate the complexity of sustainability into clear pathways for decision-making and action for both private and public sector clients. Our main analytical activities include:
  • Strategy consulting for companies, institutions, and governments
  • Data visualization for translating and communicating complex information

Design

Metabolic develops sustainable urban metabolisms and circular business models for products and services. Our work is focused primarily on:
  • Feasibility studies and designs for cleantech urban developments
  • Infrastructure design, investment, and transition plans for cities and regions
  • Integrated product design for startups that see us as partners

Experiment

Research and development is core to our work. Understanding local context, testing the efficiency of integrated systems, and experimenting with new materials and technologies.
  • Sociological studies
  • Research and testing cleantech systems
  • Maximizing synergies of energy and nutrients for complex urban farming systems
  • DIY technology and materials workshops

Build

We strive to take projects all the way through to execution. Much of our work starts out as a study or design, but ultimately ends up with creating something tangible. That work includes:
  • Cleantech installations
  • Micro-utility development
  • Infrastructure and technology
  • Products for consumers and businesses
  • Polyculture plant and food production systems
